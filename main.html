Chrome restart

Instructions

Unfortunately, apps cannot restart browser - users have to do it themselves. To restart your browser,
  • type "chrome://restart" in the address line (you can copy it from here and paste there)
  • hit Enter on the keyboard or select the address line suggested by Chrome
  • wait a little - it takes a moment or two
